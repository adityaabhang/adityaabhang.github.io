Curve Tracing

Inverse KinematicsPath FollowingInterpolationEnd-EffectorPython
Project image here (760×360)

Overview

This lab challenged me to make a robotic manipulator trace complex curves in Cartesian space with its end-effector. Using inverse kinematics and fine-grained interpolation, I programmed the robot to follow circular arcs, figure-eight patterns, and custom spline curves while maintaining consistent tool orientation and velocity throughout the path.

Methodology

I discretized target curves into dense waypoint sequences, solved inverse kinematics at each point to find valid joint configurations, applied interpolation for smooth transitions, and implemented velocity scaling to maintain constant end-effector speed along the curve regardless of the path's curvature. Singularity detection and avoidance were also addressed.
